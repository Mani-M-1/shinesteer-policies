Refund and Cancellation Policy

Effective Date: July 4, 2025

This Refund and Cancellation Policy explains how cancellations and refunds are handled for purchases made through the Shinesteer app operated by SYTY INFOTECH SOLUTIONS PRIVATE LIMITED.

1. Order Cancellations

  • Orders can be cancelled within 7 days of placement, provided the order hasn’t been dispatched or processed by the seller.
  • If the order is already out for delivery, you may reject it upon arrival.
  • Cancellation is not accepted for perishable items unless the product is damaged or defective.

2. Refund Conditions

  • If a product is damaged, defective, or not as described, you must report it within 7 days of receipt.
  • All refund claims are subject to validation and approval by the concerned seller or service provider.
  • Refunds for approved requests will be processed within 2 working days.

3. Manufacturer Warranties

For products covered under manufacturer warranty, users should contact the manufacturer directly.
