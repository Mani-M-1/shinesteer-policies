Refund and Cancellation Policy

Effective Date: July 4, 2025

This Refund and Cancellation Policy applies to purchases made through the Shinesteer app, operated by SYTY INFOTECH SOLUTIONS PRIVATE LIMITED.

1. Scope

All provisions below apply only to intangible services (e.g., consultations, digital subscriptions, online courses, maintenance plans) sold via Shinesteer. Physical goods are excluded from this policy.

2. Cancellations

  • Pre-Service Cancellation: You may cancel any service request up to 48 hours before the scheduled service start date/time, without penalty.
  • Late Cancellation: Cancellations made less than 48 hours before the scheduled start are non-refundable and will incur the full service fee.

3. Refunds

  • General Rule: All services are provided “as is” once delivered; therefore, refunds are not applicable after service completion.
  • Approval & Processing: If your refund request is approved, refund amount will be credited back to original mode of payment within 7-10 business days.

4. Modifications to Scheduled Services

  • You may request to reschedule a booked service up to 24 hours before the original start time, subject to provider availability.
  • Rescheduling requests made with less than 24 hours’ notice may incur a 25% rescheduling fee of the service cost.

5. Manufacturer and Third-Party Warranties

Not applicable—this policy covers only service transactions.
