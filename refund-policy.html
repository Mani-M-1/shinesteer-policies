Refund Policy

1. Overview

Shinesteer is committed to customer satisfaction with our home car wash services. This Refund Policy outlines when and how refunds are issued for bookings.

2. Eligibility for Refunds

  • You cancel your booking at least 2 hours before the cleaner’s scheduled arrival.
  • The cleaner fails to arrive at your provided address due to our error.
  • The service was not performed satisfactorily due to the cleaner’s negligence.

3. Non-Refundable Situations

  • You cancel less than 2 hours before the scheduled service.
  • The cleaner completes the service as agreed at your provided address.
  • Vehicle damage was pre-existing or not caused by our cleaner.

4. Refund Process

  • Email support@shinesteer.com with your booking ID and issue details.
  • Approved refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days to your original payment method.

5. Contact Us

For refund inquiries, contact us at support@shinesteer.com.
